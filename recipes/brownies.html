Brownies Recipe

Description

Homemade Brownie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Powdered sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder (which contains cornstarch)
  • Canola Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Mix together the dry and wet ingredients in two separate bowls
  2. Next combine the wet and dry ingredients
  3. Pour the batter into an 8x8 inch baking pan line with parchment paper
  4. Transfer the pan to a 325 deg F oven and bake for 40 to 45 minutes
